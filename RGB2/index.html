Mixing Light
You have three flashlights: one red, one green, and one blue. When turned on, they shine circles of light that overlap, creating new colors. For example, red and green make yellow. When all three overlap, you get a mix of red, green, and blue.

Each flashlight has a brightness slider. If a flashlight is off, it shows black to show the absence of light.

Try it out!
Use the sliders to adjust brightness and see how the lights mix. Play around to create different colors from the combination of red, green, and blue lights!
